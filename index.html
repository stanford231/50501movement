About the Movement

The 50501 Movement is a grassroots resistance effort against authoritarianism and injustice. We demand the restoration of democratic values, protection of marginalized communities, and accountability for corruption.

  • Reject Trump and Vance's policies
  • Protect LGBTQ+ and minority rights
  • Reinstate DEI initiatives
  • Defend Ukraine and democratic allies
  • End misuse of power and censorship

Get Involved
